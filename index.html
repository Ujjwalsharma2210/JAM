Welcome to Student Grievance Support


This is a place for students to solve their problems and get help.

This is a web based application created to support students who are unable to share the problems related to their institution, which can be faculty related, fee related, academics related, etcetera.We preferred web based application because of it's accessibility, ease of use and simple user interface.Students stay anonymous and the secure authentication process enables us to separate fake from the genuine grievances.
